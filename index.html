Junior Colourist / Colour Assist

Full Time Bermondsey, London

About DOMA

DOMA is an emerging London-based post-production company specialising in colour grading, editing, and VFX for brands, agencies, and music clients. Founded by Jake Evans and Morgan Faverty, who previously built Pavilion Works into a leading social-first production company, DOMA was built on a dedication to contemporary craft and emerging technologies within our fast moving industry.

We are a tight-knit team based in Bermondsey, working across a broad range of industries and clients including the likes of OpenAI, Spotify, On, and Footlocker — collaborating with leading agencies and production companies in the process. We invest in emerging talent and are constantly investigating new technologies, as part of how we can continue to evolve and deliver our work to the highest possible level.

We are looking to expand our colour department and are on the hunt for a Junior Colourist / Colour Assistant to join the team. This is a hybrid role for someone with genuine grading ambition who wants real desk time and a clear path to their own projects. You will work closely with our existing roster of colourists, take on colour assist responsibilities and data management, as well as grading your own jobs as your skills develop.

The Role

  • Assist the senior colourist on high-end commercial and music video projects
  • Manage data and media on incoming and outgoing jobs
  • Conform, prep, and organise timelines in DaVinci Resolve
  • Run colour sessions and liaise with clients and directors when required
  • Take on your own grading projects around assisting responsibilities
  • Support the wider team with suite operations when clients are in

What We're Looking For

  • Solid working knowledge of DaVinci Resolve is a must
  • Working knowledge of Premiere Pro — not essential but preferred
  • Familiarity with professional camera formats
  • Strong understanding of codecs and delivery formats
  • Knowledge of broadcast and online delivery specs
  • Solid media management skills: ingest, transcode, conform, and archive
  • Openness to AI-assisted tools and evolving colour workflows
  • Experience in a post-production environment, ideally as a colour assist or junior colourist
  • An eye for detail and a genuine passion for the craft of colour grading
  • Comfortable working across multiple projects in a fast-moving environment
  • A collaborative, team-first attitude and strong communication skills
  • Someone keen to develop their own client base and help grow the colour department
